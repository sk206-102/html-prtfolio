The Best Movies According to Sathvika

My top 3 movies of all time:

3 Idiots

A hilarious Hindi movie where three friends navigate college life, challenging the system and discovering their true passions, all while delivering humor, heart, and a powerful message about following your dreams.


F.R.I.E.N.D.S

This sitcom follows the merry misadventures of six 20-something pals as they navigate work, life and love in Manhattan.


To All the Boys I've Loved Before

A funny rom-com following Lara Jean, who writes letters to her crushes and gets into a fake relationship with Peter, one of the recipients.
